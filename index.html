!NEW! Final Forms !MUST COMPLETE!

Greencastle has implemented a new system called Final Forms. This will help keep track of physicals, dues, signatures and so on.
Personally I am very excited for this new feature. It will help me in more ways than one with organizing information from the players and parents.
With change comes confusion. Below I have included a step by step guide on how to get your account set up.

  1. Select the Link to get started -- Final Forms Sign up
  2. Choose Parent or Student for the one that applies to you and select "New Account"
  3. Fill out your information.
  4. Next it will prompt you to check your email for a verification.
  5. Open the email from Final Forms. It will give you a verfiy your account.
  6. Enter in your password and confirm your account.
  7. If all went well it should pull up instrcutional information on how the site works.
